## Core Analogy Equations Covered

1. **Reynolds Analogy** (1874): St = f/2 (Pr=Sc=1)
2. **Chilton-Colburn Analogy** (1933): j_H = j_D = f/2 with Pr^(2/3), Sc^(2/3) corrections
3. **Prandtl-Taylor Analogy** (1910s): Two-layer turbulent model
4. **von Karman Analogy** (1930s): Three-layer turbulent model
5. **Lyon-Martinelli** (1950s): Liquid metal correlation (Pr << 1)
6. **Blasius Solution** (1908): Laminar flat-plate BL
7. **Pohlhausen Solution** (1921): Thermal BL
8. **Dittus-Boelter** (1930): Turbulent pipe correlation
9. **Gnielinski** (1976): Improved turbulent correlation
10. **Colebrook-White** (1939): Turbulent friction factor
