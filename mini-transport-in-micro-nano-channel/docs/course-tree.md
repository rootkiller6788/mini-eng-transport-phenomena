# Course Tree: Transport in Micro/Nano Channels

## Prerequisites
1. Fluid Mechanics (Navier-Stokes equations, Poiseuille flow)
2. Heat Transfer (conduction, convection basics)
3. Electrostatics (Poisson equation, Debye screening)
4. Physical Chemistry (electrolyte solutions, ion transport)
5. Statistical Mechanics (kinetic theory, Boltzmann distribution)

## Dependencies
- mini-eng-fluid-mechanics (Poiseuille flow, dimensionless numbers)
- mini-eng-heat-mass-transfer (transport coefficients)
- mini-eng-electromagnetics (Poisson equation, electrostatics)
- mini-eng-solid-mechanics-vibration (channel fabrication context)

## Post-requisites
- mini-eng-multiphysics-coupling (electrokinetic-flow coupling)
- mini-eng-phase-interface-physics (surface charge, EDL)
